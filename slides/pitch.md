# Their problem

- Hospitals often have no blood in stock
- Families must go find donors themselves
- They post on Facebook, call everyone, lose hours
- The right donor may be 2 km away — but there's no fast way to reach them

---

<!-- slide 3 -->

# What I built

**Blood Help** — a free web app (PWA)

- Connects a request to nearby matching donors in **minutes**
- Burmese-first, privacy-first
- Tap "I'll help" → call → meet → confirm with a QR scan
- Closes when done, so no one travels for nothing
